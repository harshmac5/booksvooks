About BooksVooks

Connecting students to share knowledge and save money

Our Mission

BooksVooks was created with a simple mission: to make education more affordable and accessible for all students. We believe that the high cost of textbooks and study materials should never be a barrier to learning.

By creating a platform where students can rent and sell books directly to each other, we're building a community that values knowledge sharing, sustainability, and financial responsibility.

Our Story

2021

The Idea

BooksVooks started as a college project by three Delhi University students who were frustrated with the high cost of textbooks. They envisioned a platform where students could help each other by sharing resources.

2022

The Launch

After months of development, BooksVooks was launched at Delhi University, quickly gaining popularity among students looking for affordable textbooks and study materials.

2023

Expansion

BooksVooks expanded to universities across Delhi NCR, helping thousands of students save money on their educational materials while promoting sustainability through reuse.

Today

Growing Community

Today, BooksVooks is a thriving community of students across India, sharing not just textbooks but also notes, novels, and other educational resources. We continue to grow with the mission of making education more accessible for all.
