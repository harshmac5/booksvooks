Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about BooksVooks

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of any page. Fill in your details including your name, email, and password. Verify your email address through the link we send you, and you're all set!

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take data security very seriously. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal information. We never share your data with third parties without your consent. You can read more about our data practices in our Privacy Policy.

How can I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Click the link in your email and follow the instructions to create a new password.

How do I update my profile information?

Log in to your account and click on your profile icon. Select "Edit Profile" to update your personal information, contact details, and preferences. Don't forget to click "Save Changes" when you're done.

How do I purchase a book?

To purchase a book, browse our listings or search for a specific title. When you find a book you want to buy, click on it to view details. Select the "Buy" option and click "Buy Now". You'll be prompted to arrange payment and delivery/pickup with the seller through our secure messaging system.

What if the book I receive is not as described?

If you receive a book that doesn't match the seller's description, please contact the seller directly through our messaging system within 48 hours of receiving the book. If you can't resolve the issue with the seller, our support team can help mediate the dispute. You can contact us at support@booksvooks.com.

What delivery options are available?

We offer several delivery options: in-person pickup (recommended for local transactions), standard shipping (5-7 business days), and express shipping (2-3 business days). The available options and costs will be displayed during checkout and may vary depending on your location and the seller's preferences.

Can I track my book delivery?

Yes! Once your book is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email and in your order details. You can track your package's status through our website or the courier's tracking system. We'll also send you notifications about important delivery updates.

What condition are the books in?

Sellers must accurately describe their books' conditions using our standardized ratings: New, Like New, Very Good, Good, and Acceptable. Each listing includes detailed information about the book's condition, including any highlighting, notes, or wear. Photos of the actual book are also provided to help you make an informed decision.

How does the book rental process work?

Renting books is easy! Browse our rental listings and select a book you want to rent. Choose your preferred rental period (monthly, semester, or yearly). Pay the rental fee plus a refundable security deposit. After the rental period ends, return the book in good condition to get your deposit back.

What happens if I damage a rented book?

We understand that normal wear and tear happens during use. However, if a book is returned with significant damage (water damage, missing pages, broken spine, excessive highlighting), a portion or all of your security deposit may be withheld. The exact amount depends on the severity of the damage and will be determined by the book owner.

What are the available rental periods?

We offer flexible rental periods to suit your needs: Monthly (30 days), Semester (4 months), and Yearly (12 months). You can also extend your rental period if needed by contacting the book owner through our messaging system before the rental period ends.

How much is the security deposit?

The security deposit typically ranges from 30% to 50% of the book's current market value. This amount is fully refundable when you return the book in good condition within the rental period. The exact deposit amount will be clearly shown before you complete the rental transaction.

Can I return a book before the rental period ends?

Yes, you can return a book early! However, rental fees are calculated based on the initially selected rental period. For early returns, contact the book owner through our messaging system to arrange the return. Your security deposit will be refunded, but the rental fee remains the same.

How do I list a book for sale or rent?

To list a book, click on "Sell" in the navigation menu. Fill out the listing form with details about your book including title, author, condition, and price. Upload clear photos of the book. Choose whether you want to list it for sale, rent, or both. Submit your listing, and it will be live on our platform after a quick review.

How much should I charge for my books?

When setting your price, consider the book's original price, condition, demand, and edition. For textbooks, newer editions typically command higher prices. For rentals, monthly rates are usually 10-15% of the book's value. Browse similar listings to get an idea of competitive pricing. Remember that reasonably priced books tend to sell faster!

What are the requirements for book photos?

Upload clear, well-lit photos that accurately show the book's condition. Include pictures of: front and back covers, spine, any damage or wear, table of contents, and a sample page. Photos should be in focus and taken against a plain background. Good quality photos increase your chances of selling!

How do I handle shipping and delivery?

You can choose to offer shipping, in-person delivery, or both. For shipping, package the book securely to prevent damage. Use tracking services for security. You're responsible for shipping within 2 business days after receiving payment. For local transactions, arrange a safe public meeting place through our messaging system.

What happens if a buyer claims the book is damaged?

If a buyer reports damage, they must provide photos within 48 hours of receiving the book. We'll review the case and compare with your listing photos. If the damage occurred during shipping, the shipping insurance will cover it. For disputes, our support team will mediate and help reach a fair resolution.

What payment methods are accepted?

BooksVooks supports various payment methods including UPI, net banking, and major credit/debit cards. For in-person exchanges, cash payments are also an option. We recommend using our secure messaging system to arrange payment details with the other party.

Does BooksVooks charge any fees?

BooksVooks charges a small service fee of 5% on each successful transaction to maintain our platform and provide secure services. This fee is automatically calculated and deducted from the final sale price. There are no fees for listing books or browsing the platform.

How do refunds work?

Refunds can be initiated within 48 hours of receiving a book if it doesn't match the listing description. For rental security deposits, refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after the book is returned in good condition. All refunds are sent back to the original payment method used for the transaction.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and secure payment gateways to process all transactions. We never store your complete credit card information on our servers. All payment processing is handled by trusted third-party payment processors that comply with PCI DSS standards.

When do I get paid for my book sales?

After a successful sale and delivery confirmation from the buyer, the payment (minus our service fee) will be transferred to your linked bank account within 5-7 business days. For rental income, payments are processed at the start of each rental period.

Our support team is here to help with any other questions you might have.
