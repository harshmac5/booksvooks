Frequently Asked Questions

How does the rental process work?

Our rental process is simple: find a book you need, contact the owner through our platform, agree on rental terms, meet to exchange the book and security deposit, and return the book when the rental period ends.

What if the book gets damaged?

You're responsible for returning the book in the same condition you received it. Minor wear is expected, but significant damage may result in partial or full loss of your security deposit.

Can I extend my rental period?

Yes, you can request an extension by contacting the book owner. If they agree, you can update the rental agreement and pay for the additional time.

How much is the security deposit?

Security deposits vary by book but typically range from 50-70% of the book's current market value. This deposit is fully refundable when you return the book in good condition.

What happens if I return the book late?

Late returns may incur additional rental fees as specified in your rental agreement. It's important to communicate with the book owner if you need more time.
