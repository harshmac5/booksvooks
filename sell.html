Frequently Asked Questions

How do I price my books?

Consider the original price, condition, demand, and check similar listings. Generally, used textbooks sell for 40-70% of the original price.

Is there a fee for listing?

No, listing your books on BooksVooks is completely free.

How do I handle the exchange?

You can arrange to meet the buyer on campus or use our secure exchange points. Always prioritize safety.
